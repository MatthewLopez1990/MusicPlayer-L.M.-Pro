# Privacy Policy for MusicPlayer-L.M. Pro

**Last Updated:** January 19, 2026

## 1. Overview
MusicPlayer-L.M. Pro ("the App") is a local music player designed to play audio files stored on your device. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it.

## 2. Data Collection
**We do not collect, store, or share any personal data.**
*   **No Analytics:** We do not track how you use the app.
*   **No Accounts:** You do not need to create an account to use the App.
*   **No Remote Servers:** All processing (audio analysis, visualization, equalization) happens locally on your device.

## 3. File Access
The App requests access to audio files on your device solely for the purpose of playback and visualization.
*   We use Apple's secure file picker (`UIDocumentPicker`) to allow you to select music.
*   The App only accesses files you explicitly select.
*   We do not upload your files to any server.
